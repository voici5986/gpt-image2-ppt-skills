## 设计令牌
- colors: Deep navy base, vibrant magenta/pink, warm orange/yellow, cool mint/teal, stark white.
- fonts: Condensed bold sans-serif for display, elegant serif for body.
- spacing: Generous padding inside white content blocks; edge-to-edge bleed for background shapes.
- shape_language: Oversized organic/curved shapes mixed with rigid, sharp-edged rectangles and squares.
- texture: Smooth, flat vector gradients; no grain or noise.
- grid: Modular grid for multi-column cards; center-axis for covers.
- motion_or_depth: Flat design with depth created purely through overlapping opaque layers and stark contrast.

## 基础提示词模板

你是一位资深演示文稿视觉设计师。请生成 16:9 横版幻灯片，并使用「渐变有机植物风 / Flowery Google Slides and PowerPoint Template」风格。只能迁移抽象设计规律，不得复刻任何来源模板页面或素材。

【核心视觉】
- Oversized, overlapping organic shapes resembling abstracted foliage.
- Smooth, multi-color gradients within the organic shapes (e.g., purple to pink, yellow to orange, teal to mint).
- Stark white, opaque geometric blocks (squares and rectangles) used as text containers on busy backgrounds.
- Organic shapes intruding from the edges and corners of the slide to frame the content.
- 推荐配色：#1A0B40、#E83A59、#FFB347、#48C9B0、#FFFFFF、#3F51B5

【字体】
- Headings: Bold, tall, condensed sans-serif, often colored in vibrant pink/red or deep navy.
- Body: Clean, legible serif or light sans-serif, providing elegant contrast to the bold headings.
- Alignment: Center alignment for covers and quotes; left or right alignment within large horizontal content blocks.

【封面页构图】
- Background fully covered by overlapping, vibrant gradient organic shapes on a deep navy base.
- A perfectly centered, stark white square block in the middle of the slide.
- Title and subtitle text centered within the white square, using a bold accent color for the main title.

【内容页构图】
- Deep navy background with organic shapes framing the edges.
- A large, solid white horizontal rectangle spanning from one edge to the center, or floating in the middle.
- Text placed inside the white rectangle for maximum readability, aligned to the side with the most white space.

【布局系统】
- cover: Centered white square on full-bleed organic background.
- agenda: Large white horizontal band across a dark organic background, text aligned to one side.
- section: Asymmetrical organic background with a large white rectangular text container anchored to one edge.
- content: White background with corner organic accents, using solid colored rectangular cards for text.
- comparison: Two large, equal-sized solid colored rectangles (e.g., orange and pink) side-by-side on a white background.
- data: Three-column layout using tall, solid colored rectangular cards (e.g., mint, blue, red) with white icons and text.
- quote: White background, organic shapes on one side, quote text enclosed in a thin, colored rectangular outline.
- closing: Return to the cover layout pattern for visual closure.

【图片处理】
- Images are generally avoided in favor of the strong vector graphic style.
- If used, images should be cropped into rigid geometric shapes (squares/rectangles) to contrast with the organic background.
- Apply a subtle duotone or color overlay matching the palette to integrate photos.

【图标与装饰】
- Simple, thin white line icons.
- Centered at the top of solid-colored rectangular cards.
- No fill colors or complex details within the icons.

【数据页构图】
- Clean white background with minimal organic shapes peeking from the corners.
- Data visualized using flat, solid colors drawn from the vibrant gradient palette.
- Clear, bold typography for key metrics, housed within solid colored rectangular cards.

【图表风格】
- Flat, 2D charts using the vibrant accent colors (pink, orange, mint, blue).
- No 3D effects, gradients, or shadows on chart elements.
- Clean white or deep navy backgrounds for charts to ensure data legibility.
- Simple, bold sans-serif fonts for axes and data labels.

【章节页构图】
- Similar to the cover layout but with a slightly asymmetrical arrangement of the background shapes.
- A central or slightly offset white square or rectangle containing the section title and a brief description.

【收尾页构图】
- Full bleed organic gradient shapes on a dark background.
- A central white square containing a bold 'Thank You' or closing statement.
- Contact information neatly formatted in serif typography below the main heading.

【禁止】
- Realistic photography of plants or leaves.
- Drop shadows, bevels, or 3D effects on shapes and text.
- Placing dark or thin text directly over the busy gradient backgrounds without a container.
- Muted, pastel, or desaturated color palettes.
- Specific leaf or plant illustrations.
- Exact text placeholders like 'HELLO CREATIVITY!' or 'WHOA!'.
- Exact gradient angle and color stop combinations from the source images.
- 禁止出现来源网站 UI、水印、下载提示或模板署名。
