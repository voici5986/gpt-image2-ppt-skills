- Thin vertical line accents used for framing edges, separating numbers from text, and anchoring paragraphs
- High-contrast typographic hierarchy
- Generous use of negative space to create a luxurious, unhurried pace
- 推荐配色：Primary Background: Off-white/Cream (e.g., #F4F0EB) for a premium, tactile feel、Primary Text & Accents: Dark Charcoal/Soft Black (e.g., #1A1A1A)、Subtle Accents: Muted Olive Green/Khaki (e.g., #5A6347) used sparingly for numbers or highlights

【字体】
- Headers & Numerals: Oversized, high-contrast, elegant display serif (incorporating ligatures or alternates if available)
- Body & Captions: Clean, highly legible, modern sans-serif
- Hierarchy: Extreme contrast in scale between massive serif headers/numbers and small sans-serif body copy

【封面页构图】
- Split layout utilizing negative space
- Left side: Large serif title and small sans-serif subtitle aligned to the left
- Right side: Tall vertical image crop (approx 1/3 width) spanning top to bottom
- Far right edge: Thin vertical framing line and a circled date/number badge

【内容页构图】
- Asymmetrical balance between text and imagery
- Text block on one side, often featuring a thin vertical line accent to the left of the paragraph
- Opposite side features a tall vertical image crop (1/3 or 1/4 width)
- Far edge often framed by a single thin vertical line

【布局系统】

- Comparison/Data: Matrix created by thin intersecting horizontal and vertical lines
