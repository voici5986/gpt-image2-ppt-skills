【核心视觉】
- High-contrast typographic pairing (ultra-bold sans-serif + formal script)
- Oversized slash-numbering (e.g., /001)
- Underscore prefix for secondary title lines (e.g., _TITLE)
- Abundant negative space with a strict minimalist grid
- Bottom-anchored minimalist footer
- 推荐配色：#F7F7F5、#1A1A1A、#88B04B

【字体】
- Primary Titles: Ultra-bold, all-caps brutalist sans-serif
- Accent Words: Delicate, formal script font
- Body Text: Clean, highly legible geometric sans-serif
- Numbering: Oversized, ultra-bold sans-serif with a forward slash prefix

【封面页构图】
- Split layout with massive typography on the left and a large vertical image placeholder on the right
- Title uses the signature sans-serif + script pairing with an underscore prefix
- Small, discreet subtitle in the top right corner
- Minimalist footer anchored to the bottom edge (Left: Title, Center: Date, Right: Year)

【内容页构图】
- Asymmetrical balance utilizing heavy negative space
- Titles often positioned on the left (top or bottom) using the signature typographic pairing
- Body text constrained to specific grid columns, often on the right or top
- Footer and slide number anchored to the bottom corners

【布局系统】
- 复用稳定页面骨架：封面、目录、章节、内容、对比、数据、引用、收尾都应共享相同网格和视觉节奏。

【图片处理】
- Images placed in strict, unrounded rectangular placeholders
- Vertical portrait orientation preferred for main feature images
- No borders, shadows, or frames around images
- High-contrast, artistic, or sculptural imagery fits best

【图标与装饰】
- Minimalist, line-art style icons
- Used sparingly, primarily for instructional or data slides
- Icons placed above text blocks with generous padding

【数据页构图】
- Clean, grid-based alignment for lists and data points
- Oversized slash-numbers used as bullet points or list markers
- Generous vertical spacing between data rows
- Accent colors (like muted green) used sparingly for highlights or bounding boxes

【图表风格】
- Flat, minimalist charts
- Monochrome or utilizing the single muted accent color
- No 3D effects, gridlines kept to an absolute minimum
- Typography in charts matches the clean geometric sans-serif body font

【章节页构图】
