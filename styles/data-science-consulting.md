## 基础提示词模板

你是一位资深演示文稿视觉设计师。请生成 16:9 横版幻灯片，并使用「数据科学咨询演示模板 / Data Science Consulting Presentation Template」风格。只能迁移抽象设计规律，不得复刻任何来源模板页面或素材。

【核心视觉】
- Sparse floating small squares (solid and hollow)
- Thin vertical and horizontal connecting lines
- Icons housed within solid colored squares
- Dark background with high-contrast vibrant accents
- 推荐配色：Dark Navy (Background)、Cyan (Accent/Highlight)、Peach (Accent)、Light Pink (Accent)、White (Primary Text)

【字体】
- Headers: Condensed, uppercase sans-serif (White or Cyan)
- Body: Clean, highly legible standard sans-serif (White or Light Gray)
- Numbers: Large, stylized condensed sans-serif

【封面页构图】
- Centered, large uppercase title (mixed white and cyan)
- Centered subtitle below
- Background decorated with thin vertical lines terminating in small colored squares (falling data effect)

【内容页构图】
- Left-aligned header
- Multi-column text blocks or numbered lists
- Corner-anchored floating square motifs

【布局系统】
- 复用稳定页面骨架：封面、目录、章节、内容、对比、数据、引用、收尾都应共享相同网格和视觉节奏。

【图片处理】
- No photos used; relies entirely on vector shapes, line-art icons, and typography.

【图标与装饰】
- Simple, black line-art icons
- Placed centrally inside solid colored squares (cyan, peach, pink)

【数据页构图】
- Custom rounded horizontal bar charts
- Stepped pyramid charts split symmetrically
- Thin lines connecting data points to explanatory text

【图表风格】
- Horizontal bar charts with fully rounded ends
- Stepped pyramid charts
- Thin vertical grid lines
- No axes lines, clean minimalist look

【章节页构图】
- Centered or left-aligned large title
- Large numbered square connected to a horizontal colored line (progress bar effect)
- Floating square background motif

【收尾页构图】
- Centered thank you message
- Contact information in a clean grid
- Consistent falling square background motif

【禁止】
- Complex photographic backgrounds
- 3D realistic elements
- Serif fonts
- Cluttered layouts
- Gradients (stick to solid colors)
- Specific text content
- Exact fingerprint graphic
- Specific numerical data
- 禁止出现来源网站 UI、水印、下载提示或模板署名。

## 适用场景
Data Science Pitches、Tech Consulting Reports、IT Strategy Presentations、Software Architecture Overviews。
