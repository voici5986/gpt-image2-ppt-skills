几何商务风 / Geometric Business

## 风格描述
A modern, professional presentation style characterized by bold, oversized geometric shapes—such as intersecting diagonal bands, triangles, and segmented concentric circles. It uses high-contrast color blocking to frame clean white spaces for typography and content, ideal for corporate and business school presentations.

## 设计令牌
- colors: High-contrast corporate palette: Crisp White, Charcoal Black, Slate Grey-Blue, Navy, Medium Blue.
- fonts: Modern sans-serif pairing: Bold/condensed for headings, clean/neutral for body.
- spacing: Generous padding within white text containers; edge-to-edge bleeding for geometric background shapes.
- shape_language: Sharp angles, intersecting diagonals, triangles, and segmented concentric arcs.
- texture: Flat, solid color fills with no gradients or textures.
- grid: Modular grid with strong diagonal or circular focal points breaking the standard orthogonal layout.
- motion_or_depth: Flat 2D design with depth implied only through overlapping solid shapes.

【核心视觉】
- Oversized intersecting diagonal bands and triangles
- Segmented concentric circles acting as framing devices or image masks
- High-contrast color blocking
- Clean white central or horizontal bands for text framing
- 推荐配色：#FFFFFF、#1E2227、#A9BCC2、#1A4685、#3B76D3

【字体】
- Primary: Bold, slightly condensed sans-serif for headings
- Secondary: Clean, highly legible sans-serif for body text
- High contrast text colors (black/dark grey on white backgrounds, white on dark bands)

【封面页构图】
- Large geometric shapes (diagonals or partial circles) anchoring the corners or edges
- A prominent white negative space area (central rectangle or left-aligned block) for the main title
- Optional circular image mask integrated into the geometric pattern on one side

【内容页构图】
- Horizontal white band across the middle for text, framed by diagonal geometric shapes at the top and bottom
- Clean white background with subtle geometric accents in the corners to maximize content area

【布局系统】
- 复用稳定页面骨架：封面、目录、章节、内容、对比、数据、引用、收尾都应共享相同网格和视觉节奏。

【图片处理】
- Images masked within perfect circles or sharp geometric polygons
- No borders or drop shadows on images
- Use of professional, well-lit corporate photography

【图标与装饰】
- Flat, minimalist vector icons
- Monocolor or dual-color using the primary palette
- Sharp, geometric icon construction matching the overall shape language

【数据页构图】
- Charts and graphs placed within clean white containers
- Data points highlighted using the primary palette colors (navy, slate, medium blue)
- Minimalist axes and gridlines to maintain the clean geometric aesthetic

【图表风格】
- Flat design with solid colors
- No 3D effects or gradients
- Use slate, navy, and medium blue for data series
- Clean, sans-serif labels

【章节页构图】
- High-contrast background using a primary dark color (black or navy) with intersecting lighter shapes
- Large, bold section title centered or aligned to a geometric intersection
- Subtitles placed in a contrasting white horizontal band or box

【收尾页构图】
- Symmetrical geometric framing (e.g., intersecting diagonals pointing towards the center)
- Central white space containing a bold closing statement or call to action
- Contact information neatly aligned below the main heading

【禁止】
- Organic, hand-drawn, or watercolor shapes
- Cluttered layouts with insufficient negative space
- Low-contrast text on busy backgrounds
- Drop shadows, gradients, or 3D bevel effects on geometric shapes
- Decorative serif fonts
- Exact text like 'Here is where your presentation begins' or 'Whoa!'
- Specific stock photos of hands stacked together
- The exact watercolor doodle from the anomalous reference image
