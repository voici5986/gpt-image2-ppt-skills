## 设计令牌
- colors: Primary: Coral；Secondary: Deep Purple；Accent: White
- fonts: Header: Bold Condensed Sans-serif；Body: Clean Serif/Sans-serif
- spacing: Wide margins；High contrast in scale between headers and body text；Tight tracking on headers
- shape_language: Sharp rectangles；Perfect circles for diagrams；Strict orthogonal lines
- texture: Flat solid colors；Monochrome/duotone photographic textures
- grid: 50/50 vertical and horizontal splits；2-column and 3-column strict grids；Visible grid lines (1px/2px)
- motion_or_depth: Flat design；Depth achieved only through overlapping text on images

## 基础提示词模板

你是一位资深演示文稿视觉设计师。请生成 16:9 横版幻灯片，并使用「创意机构双色调演示模板 / Creative Agency Duotone Presentation Template」风格。只能迁移抽象设计规律，不得复刻任何来源模板页面或素材。

【核心视觉】
- Strict duotone color scheme
- Oversized, bold, condensed typography for headers
- 50/50 vertical and horizontal split layouts
- Structural 1px/2px accent lines for framing and dividing
- Overlapping text-on-image techniques
- 推荐配色：Coral (#FF7A59)、Deep Purple (#6A4C79)、White (#FFFFFF)

【字体】
- Headers: Oversized, bold, condensed sans-serif, uppercase
- Body: Clean, legible serif or sans-serif, small size for high contrast
- Numbers: Oversized, matching header font style

【封面页构图】
- 50/50 vertical split layout
- Solid color block on one side, duotone image on the other
- Massive uppercase title on the solid side
- Thin horizontal divider line separating title and subtitle

【内容页构图】
- Overlapping oversized headers that cross the boundary between image and solid background
- Multi-column text blocks separated by thin vertical lines
- Consistent thin accent line on the far right edge of the slide

【布局系统】
- 复用稳定页面骨架：封面、目录、章节、内容、对比、数据、引用、收尾都应共享相同网格和视觉节奏。

【图片处理】
- Strict purple monochrome or duotone filter applied to all photographs
- Images used as structural blocks (50/50 splits or grid squares) rather than floating elements

【图标与装饰】
- Minimalist, outline style
- Monocolor (matching the contrasting palette color)
- Used sparingly as bullet points or timeline markers

【数据页构图】
- Matrix layouts using thin intersecting lines and geometric shapes
- 2x3 checkerboard grids alternating solid color blocks and duotone images
- Horizontal timelines connected by thin 1px/2px lines

【图表风格】
- Flat, geometric construction
- Built with thin 1px/2px lines
- Strictly adhering to the coral/purple/white palette

【章节页构图】
- 50/50 vertical split with duotone image and solid color
- Oversized header text overlapping the image boundary
- Thin horizontal divider under the header

【收尾页构图】
- 50/50 split mirroring the cover layout
- Bold thank you message and contact details separated by thin lines

【禁止】
- Full-color photographs (must be duotone/monochrome)
- Soft gradients or drop shadows
- Script or handwritten fonts
- Cluttered layouts without clear grid alignment
- Curved or organic structural shapes
- Specific text content like 'HELLO WORLD' or 'WHAT SETS US APART?'
- Specific planet facts or placeholder copy
- The exact faces, locations, or objects in the source photos
- 禁止出现来源网站 UI、水印、下载提示或模板署名。
