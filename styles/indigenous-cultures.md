- Small, discrete, often underlined page numbers in the corners
- 推荐配色：Off-white/Beige (Backgrounds)、Dark Brown/Charcoal (Text on light backgrounds)、Pure White (Text on photographic backgrounds)

【字体】
- Titles: Bold, all-caps sans-serif

- Body: Clean, highly legible regular sans-serif
- Numbers: Prominent use of leading zeros (e.g., 01, 02) for lists and pagination

【封面页构图】
- Full-bleed background photograph
- Large, bold, all-caps title at the top, split by an em-dash
- A thin vertical line dividing the right third of the slide
- Subtitle placed in the bottom left corner
- Underlined page number '01' in the top right corner

【内容页构图】
- Split layout: 1/3 vertical image crop on one side, 2/3 off-white background on the other

【布局系统】
