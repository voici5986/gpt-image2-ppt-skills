【核心视觉】
- Deep navy background
- Oversized, bold sans-serif typography
- Vertical ribbed texture blocks (semi-transparent)
- Thin, oversized circular arcs in corners/edges
- Thin horizontal divider lines
- 推荐配色：#0B1D2A、#F4F1EA、#152A3B、#FFFFFF

【字体】
- Headers: Massive, bold, uppercase sans-serif (Off-White)
- Body: Clean, legible, regular weight sans-serif (Off-White)
- Numbers: Oversized, bold sans-serif matching headers

【封面页构图】
- Vertical ribbed texture block on the left edge
- Massive, centered or slightly left-aligned title in uppercase
- Thin circular arc intersecting the top edge
- Small, balanced meta-text on far left and right margins

【内容页构图】
- Asymmetrical two-column split (e.g., 1/3 title, 2/3 content)
- Ribbed texture block anchoring the title or content side
- Thin horizontal lines separating distinct list items or sections
- Generous negative space around text blocks

【布局系统】
- 复用稳定页面骨架：封面、目录、章节、内容、对比、数据、引用、收尾都应共享相同网格和视觉节奏。

【图片处理】
- Images occupy full vertical halves of the slide
- Images are overlaid with the vertical ribbed texture to integrate with the dark theme
- Desaturated or cool-toned images preferred to match the navy palette

【图标与装饰】
- Minimalist, line-art style
- Off-white color
- Often enclosed in thin, pill-shaped outlines

【数据页构图】
- Clean grid alignment
- Use of thin horizontal rules to separate data rows
- Icons enclosed in thin rounded rectangles/pill shapes for categorical data

【图表风格】
- Flat, minimalist charts
- Colors restricted to off-white and varying opacities of navy
- Thin line axes, no gridlines, no 3D effects

【章节页构图】
- Massive section title on one side
- Oversized section number on the opposite side, anchored by the ribbed texture block
- Thin circular arc intersecting the bottom edge

【收尾页构图】
- Centered massive closing statement
- Symmetrical placement of ribbed texture blocks on edges
- Thin circular arcs framing the top and bottom
