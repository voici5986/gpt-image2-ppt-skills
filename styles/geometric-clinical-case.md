【核心视觉】
- Modular geometric tile patterns acting as bold framing devices along edges or as full-bleed side panels.
- 推荐配色：Navy Blue、Light Blue、Black、White

【字体】
- Bold geometric sans-serif for headers
- Clean, highly legible sans-serif for body text
- All-caps styling for primary titles and section headers

【封面页构图】
- Top section filled with a dense geometric tile pattern; bottom section solid navy with left-aligned title, subtitle, and a thin horizontal rule.

【内容页构图】
- Solid color background with geometric motifs restricted to a specific edge (e.g., left vertical strip or top-right corner) to maximize negative space for text.

【布局系统】
- Cover: Top geometric pattern block, bottom solid color with left-aligned text.
- Agenda: Centered header with thin top/bottom rules, structured grid or numbered list below, optional bottom geometric border.
- Section: Split layout with one side dedicated to a dense geometric pattern and the other to typography.
- Content: Minimalist solid background with geometric framing on one edge (left strip, top corner).
- Comparison: Diagonal background color splits or side-by-side solid color blocks.
- Data: Flat tables and blocky flowcharts integrated seamlessly into the solid background.

【图片处理】
- Images, if used, should be cropped into strict geometric shapes (squares, circles) or placed within the modular grid to match the tile aesthetic.

【图标与装饰】
- Custom geometric icons built from the same primitive shapes (circles, squares, triangles) as the background patterns.
- Strict adherence to the 4-color palette.
- Flat, solid, and minimalist.

【数据页构图】
- Clean tables with solid color fills and thin borders; simple block-and-arrow flowcharts; diagonal background splits to separate data points.

【图表风格】
- Flat design, no 3D.
- Colors strictly limited to the 4-color palette.
- Clean lines, high contrast, sans-serif labels.

【章节页构图】
- Split screen: one half or third features a dense geometric pattern block, while the other side holds a solid background with a bold, oversized section number and title, often separated by a thin vertical line.

【收尾页构图】
- Symmetrical or heavily framed geometric borders surrounding centered closing remarks or contact information.
