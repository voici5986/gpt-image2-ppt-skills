## 设计令牌
- colors: Background: Dark Forest Green；Accents: Pastel Blue, Pastel Yellow, Pastel Pink, Pastel Green；Text: White (on dark), Dark Green (on light)
- fonts: Headings: Geometric Sans-Serif, Bold；Body: Geometric Sans-Serif, Regular
- spacing: Generous padding inside folder shapes (minimum 5% of slide width).；Overlapping elements offset by 2-5% to reveal tabs.
- shape_language: File folders with asymmetrical, rounded top tabs.；Rounded corners on the outer edges of the folders.；Straight, clean edges for photographs.
- texture: Subtle, high-resolution paper/cardstock grain applied globally.
- grid: Asymmetrical layouts driven by the physical placement of folders.；Common splits: 50/50, 60/40, or full-bleed background with a centered folder.
- motion_or_depth: Depth achieved exclusively through soft, directional drop shadows (simulating overhead lighting).

## 基础提示词模板

你是一位资深演示文稿视觉设计师。请生成 16:9 横版幻灯片，并使用「会议议程演示 / Meeting Agenda Presentation」风格。只能迁移抽象设计规律，不得复刻任何来源模板页面或素材。

【核心视觉】
- Realistic paper file folder shapes with distinct, rounded tabs.
- Subtle paper and cardstock texture overlays on all shapes and backgrounds.
- Soft, directional drop shadows creating a sense of physical depth and stacking.
- High contrast between the dark green background and pastel folder elements.
- 推荐配色：Dark Forest Green (Background): #1A5D3A、Pastel Blue: #A9D1E9、Pastel Yellow: #F4E285、Pastel Pink: #F4B6C2、Pastel Green: #7BC076、Crisp White: #FFFFFF

【字体】
- Primary Font: Clean, modern, geometric sans-serif.
- Heading Style: Bold, large, often in white on dark backgrounds or dark green on pastel backgrounds.
- Body Text: Regular weight sans-serif, highly legible, aligned left within folder shapes.

【封面页构图】
- Multiple colored file folders overlapping and cascading from top to bottom.
- Title text placed prominently on the bottom-most, largest folder.
- Subtitle or presenter info placed in the bottom corner.

【内容页构图】
- A pastel folder shape occupying 50-60% of the slide, containing text.
- A rectangular, cleanly cropped photograph occupying the remaining space.
- Alternatively, small folder icons acting as cards for grid-based content.

【布局系统】
- 复用稳定页面骨架：封面、目录、章节、内容、对比、数据、引用、收尾都应共享相同网格和视觉节奏。

【图片处理】
- Images should be rectangular with clean, straight edges.
- No borders or frames around images; they should sit flush against the background or folder edges.
- Photography should have a warm, natural lighting style to complement the tactile paper theme.

【图标与装饰】
- Minimalist, line-art icons.
- Icons placed inside miniature folder shapes to act as bullet points or grid items.
- White icons on dark backgrounds, or dark green icons on pastel backgrounds.

【数据页构图】
- A white paper sheet shape appearing to stick out from inside a colored folder.
- Data points, columns, or charts placed cleanly on the white paper area.
- Use of dark green text for high legibility on the white background.

【图表风格】
- Clean, flat charts utilizing the presentation's pastel color palette.
- Charts should be placed on white 'paper' shapes or directly on pastel folders.
- Avoid 3D charts; maintain the 2D paper aesthetic.

【章节页构图】
- Two overlapping folders in contrasting pastel colors.
- The top folder contains a large, bold section number and the section title.
- Generous negative space on the dark green background.

【收尾页构图】
- A return to the cascading folder visual from the cover.
- A simple 'Thank You' or closing remark on the primary folder.
- Contact information neatly organized within the folder boundaries.

【禁止】
- No flat vector shapes without the paper texture overlay.
- No harsh, solid black drop shadows (must be soft and diffused).
- No neon, fluorescent, or overly saturated colors.
- No 3D glossy effects, gradients, or metallic textures.
- No complex, cluttered layouts that break the physical folder metaphor.
- Do not copy the specific text content (e.g., planet names, placeholder text).
- Do not copy the exact photographs used in the reference images.
- Do not copy any specific logos or watermarks.
- 禁止出现来源网站 UI、水印、下载提示或模板署名。
